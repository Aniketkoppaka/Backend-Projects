Secrets

  • Write down one good thing that happened to you every day. It can change how you view your life over time.
  • When you're nervous before a presentation, imagine you're explaining something cool to a friend, not performing for strangers.
  • Learn to cook one dish really well. It builds confidence and makes you memorable at gatherings.
  • Keep a digital folder of compliments you receive. Revisit it on tough days to remind yourself of your worth.
  • Stretch for five minutes after waking up and before bed. It improves your posture, sleep, and energy.
  • If you're feeling overwhelmed, clean a small part of your room. Order in your space can bring clarity to your mind.
